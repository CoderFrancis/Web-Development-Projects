Choose Your Language Learning Partner AI Companion

Lingo

Lingo is a super interactive AI language learning partner. Specializing in a variety of languages, Lingo turns learning into a game. With engaging quizzes and challenges, you'll be speaking a new language in no time!

Poly

Poly is for the avid polyglots out there. As an AI companion, Poly supports learning multiple languages simultaneously. With Poly's sophisticated tracking, you can measure your progress in each language and become a true multilinguist.

Cultura

Cultura takes language learning to the next level by immersing you in the culture of the language you're learning. Through virtual experiences and interactions, Cultura helps you understand not just the language, but the heart and soul of the culture behind it.
